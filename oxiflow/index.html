§oxiflow

Generic engine for solving partial differential equations of the form:

∂u/∂t + ∇·F(u, ∇u) = S(u, x, t)

where u is a physical field (concentration, temperature, velocity…), F a flux (advective, diffusive, dispersive) and S a source or reaction term.

§Architecture — separation WHAT/HOW

The engine enforces three strictly separated responsibility levels:

TypeRole
ScenarioDeclares the problem
SolverConfigurationConfigures the solving
SolverOrchestrates execution
