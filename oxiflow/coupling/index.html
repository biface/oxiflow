§Module coupling

Multi-physics inter-domain coupling — INV-3 invariant (DD-011).

§Core principle (INV-3)

All interactions between distinct physical domains (lahar/lake, fluid/solid, column/reservoir) must go through the CouplingOperator trait with DomainId and Interface. No coupling logic is coded directly in the Solver (DD-011, J3).

§Types

TypeRole
InterfaceDescribes the boundary shared by two domains
CouplingOperatorTrait — exchanges state across an Interface

§Object safety

CouplingOperator is object-safe to support INV-4 (plugin-safe API, v2.0). It can be stored as Box<dyn CouplingOperator> in Scenario at J3.
