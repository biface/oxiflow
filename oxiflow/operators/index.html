§Module operators

Spatial discretization operators — INV-2 invariant.

§Core principle (INV-2)

Temporal integrators are decoupled from the spatial discretization scheme via the DiscreteOperator<M: Mesh> trait. No method — FD, FV or FEM — is called directly in an integrator. Each scheme is a separate trait implementation (DD-012, J4b).
